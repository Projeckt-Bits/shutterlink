# ShutterLink

A modern, feature-rich photo sharing application with advanced capabilities including raw footage rendering and professional-grade image processing.

## Overview

ShutterLink is a cutting-edge photo sharing platform designed for photographers, content creators, and photography enthusiasts. It combines the ease of social sharing with powerful professional tools, enabling users to share, edit, and render high-quality images and raw footage.

## Features

### Core Functionality
- **Photo Sharing**: Seamlessly share your photos with friends, followers, and the community
- **Social Feed**: Discover and engage with stunning photography from creators worldwide
- **User Profiles**: Showcase your portfolio with customizable profiles

### Advanced Features
- **Raw Footage Rendering**: Process and render raw camera files (CR2, NEF, ARW, etc.) with professional-grade algorithms
- **Advanced Image Processing**: 
  - High-dynamic range (HDR) processing
  - Color grading and correction
  - Noise reduction and sharpening
  - Lens distortion correction
- **Batch Processing**: Process multiple images simultaneously
- **Cloud Storage Integration**: Secure cloud storage for your original files and processed images
- **Metadata Preservation**: Maintain EXIF data and camera settings throughout the workflow

### User Experience
- **Intuitive Interface**: Clean, modern UI designed for both casual and professional users
- **Real-time Preview**: See your edits and renders in real-time
- **Cross-platform Support**: Access your photos from any device
- **Privacy Controls**: Granular privacy settings for your content
